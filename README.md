# Job Market Analysis 

## Project Overview
This project analyzes a **data science job postings dataset** to uncover key insights about job demand, required skills, salary trends, and hiring companies.

The goal of this project is to help a fictional recruitment company **DataSearch** understand job market trends and make better hiring recommendations.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze job posting trends over time
- Identify most in-demand job roles
- Discover top skills required for each role
- Understand industry hiring patterns
- Build interactive dashboards for stakeholders

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning & Preparation
- Handled missing values using **Power Query**
- Identified that:
  - ❗ **~92% of salary data (Min & Max Pay) is missing**
- Cleaned and formatted:
  - Date columns
  - Job titles
  - Skills columns
- Removed inconsistencies and null-heavy fields where necessary

---

## 📈 Key Findings

### 1. Job Posting Trends
- Job postings show an **overall upward trend over time**
- A significant drop occurred in **March 2020** due to COVID-19 impact
- Recovery followed with increasing demand in later years

---

### 2. Most In-Demand Roles
- Top roles identified:
  - Data Engineer
  - Data Scientist
  - Data Analyst

- These roles dominate the job market consistently

---

### 3. Salary Insights ⚠️
- Over **90% of salary values are missing**
- Due to this:
  - Salary-based conclusions are **not reliable**
- However:
  - Data Analysts tend to have the **lowest salary range** among the three major roles

---

### 4. Skills Analysis
- Skills demand remains **relatively consistent over time**
- No major upward or downward trend in top 10 skills

#### Key Insight:
- Stability in skill demand indicates:
  - Core technologies remain essential in the industry

---

### 5. Power BI Skill Demand
- **Power BI appears in ~5.10% of Data Scientist job postings**
- It is a **Top 25 skill**, though not as dominant as Python

---

### 6. Industry Insights
- The **Internet industry** hires the most for:
  - Mid-Senior level positions

- **Top hiring company:**
  - Toptal

---

### 7. Role Comparison
- The role most similar to a Data Analyst:
  - ✅ **Business Analyst**
- Similarity based on:
  - Skill type
  - Skill count

## 💡 Recommendations

### 1. Focus on High-Demand Roles
- Prioritize recruiting for:
  - Data Engineers
  - Data Scientists
  - Data Analysts

---

### 2. Invest in Core Skills
- Candidates should focus on:
  - Python
  - SQL
  - Power BI (emerging importance)

---

### 3. Industry Targeting
- Focus recruitment efforts on:
  - Internet/Tech industries
- These industries show **highest hiring demand**

---

### 4. Improve Salary Transparency
- Companies should:
  - Provide salary ranges in job postings
- Benefits:
  - Better candidate matching
  - Improved analytics accuracy

---

### 5. Stable Skill Demand Strategy
- Since skills demand is consistent:
  - Long-term upskilling strategies are effective
  - No need for frequent skill switching

---

### 6. Target Mid-Senior Roles
- Majority of hiring is for:
  - Mid-Senior level positions
- Recruiters should:
  - Focus on experienced candidates
